About Us

Dhandapani Rural Charitable Trust (DRCT) was established in 2005 with the mission to empower rural communities through education and skill development programs. Our organization works tirelessly to bridge the gap between urban and rural education systems.

Over the years, we have impacted thousands of lives through our various initiatives, including vocational training, adult education, and women empowerment programs. Our team consists of dedicated professionals who are committed to creating sustainable change in rural India.

We believe that education is the most powerful tool to transform lives and communities. Our approach combines traditional knowledge with modern skills to create holistic development opportunities for rural youth and adults alike.
