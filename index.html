About Our Trust

Our mission and vision

Who We Are

Dhandapani Rural Charitable Trust was established in 2005 with the aim of providing quality education and vocational training to rural youth. Over the years, we have transformed thousands of lives through our various educational initiatives.

Our Mission

To empower rural communities through education, skill development, and employment opportunities, thereby reducing urban migration and improving quality of life.

Our Schemes

Government and Trust-sponsored programs

Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY)

Skill development program for rural youth with placement assurance. Focuses on poverty reduction through employability.

Learn More

Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY)

Short-term training to help youth take up industry-relevant skill training for better livelihood.

Learn More

Tamil Nadu Skill Development Corporation (TNSDC)

State-sponsored programs focusing on local employment needs and industry requirements.

Learn More

AICTE Vocational Courses

Technical and vocational education programs approved by All India Council for Technical Education.
